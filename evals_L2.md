# 7-Dimension Consolidated QA Framework for Weekly L2 Operational Issues in Fintech and E-commerce with EU Markets Focus

## Framework Overview
This document outlines a framework consisting of 7 key dimensions aimed at addressing operational issues in the Fintech and E-commerce sectors, with a specific focus on compliance within the EU market context. Each dimension includes pass/fail criteria and role-specific examples to aid understanding and implementation.

### 1. Vertical Relevance
**Criteria:** Fintech AND E-commerce AND EU regulatory context all substantively covered.
- **Pass:** All areas are addressed with relevant examples.
- **Fail:** One or more areas lack substantial coverage.

### 2. Signal Quality
**Criteria:** Is the insight actionable and not purely observational?
- **Pass:** Insights lead to specific actions.
- **Fail:** Insights remain theoretical without actionable steps.

### 3. Source Independence
**Criteria:** At least 1 independent source, vendor data labelled.
- **Pass:** Data and insights sourced from independent entities.
- **Fail:** Reliance on single/vendor-supplied data.

### 4. Action Quality
**Criteria:** Actions are role-specific, achievable this week, not restatements of news.
- **Pass:** Actions are tailored to roles and can be implemented within the week.
- **Fail:** Actions lack specificity and are merely reiterations of existing news.

### 5. Regulatory Framing
**Criteria:** EU AI Act/GDPR/DORA translated to product decisions, not legal summaries.
- **Pass:** Clear translation into actionable product guidelines.
- **Fail:** Discussion is limited to legal theory without practical implications.

### 6. Workforce Framing
**Criteria:** If hiring/job topics present, either reframed as capability development or rejected with [workforce_anxiety_risk] flag.
- **Pass:** Topics reframed positively or marked accordingly.
- **Fail:** Negative framing or omission of workforce considerations.

### 7. Editorial Independence
**Criteria:** No vendor marketing disguised as thought leadership, no single-vendor announcements as strategic signals.
- **Pass:** Clear delineation from vendor marketing tactics.
- **Fail:** Marketing language or vendor-centric viewpoints dominate the discussion.

## Role-Specific Examples
- **Analyst Role Example:** Insights on regulatory updates affecting product features must be actionable, such as timelines for compliance updates.
- **Product Manager Example:** Actions that ensure product adaptations meet regulatory changes within the stipulated period.
