# Agent Orchestrator

## Master Scheduling and State Management Framework

This document outlines the master scheduling and state management framework for L1/L2/L3 pipelines, focusing on the following key components:

### 1. 9-State L1 Monthly Machine
- **State 1**: Initialization
- **State 2**: Data Acquisition
- **State 3**: Processing
- **State 4**: Validation
- **State 5**: Finalization
- **State 6**: Reporting
- **State 7**: Archive
- **State 8**: Rollback
- **State 9**: Handoff

### 2. Rollback Protocols
- Detailed procedures for reversing processes in case of errors, including checks and re-executions.

### 3. Regulatory Calendar
- A calendar highlighting important regulatory deadlines and compliance dates pertinent to operations.

### 4. Agent Handoff Sequence
- Step-by-step guide for effectively handing over tasks between agents to ensure continuity and accuracy.

---

This framework serves as a cohesive guide for the efficient management of the L1, L2, and L3 processes, ensuring compliance, regulatory alignment, and effective error management.
