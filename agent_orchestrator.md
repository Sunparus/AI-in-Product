# Production-Grade Orchestrator Architecture

## 1. Pipeline Registry
   - Description of the components of the pipeline that are used for orchestrating processes.

## 2. Agent Registry
   - A repository of all agents available for executing tasks and their associated capabilities.

## 3. Guard Conditions
   - Mechanisms to validate and ensure that conditions are appropriate for the execution of each process.

## 4. State Machine
   - Definition of the states that orchestrators can be in and the transitions between those states.

## 5. Rollback Strategy
   - Procedures to revert to a previous state in case of failure during execution, ensuring system robustness.

## 6. Escalation Hierarchy
   - Structure detailing how issues are escalated through different levels of management depending on severity.

## 7. Artifact Versioning
   - A system to track different versions of artifacts produced during the orchestrator’s execution, ensuring compatibility.

## 8. Observability
   - Tools and practices that allow monitoring the system, making it easy to observe and troubleshoot orchestrator processes.

## 9. Regulatory Integration
   - Considerations and implementations that ensure adherence to relevant regulations and compliance standards.

## 10. Fintech Guardrails
   - Safety measures and protocols tailored for financial technologies to mitigate risks and uphold integrity within processes.
