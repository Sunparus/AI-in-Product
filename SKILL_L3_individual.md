# 5-Stage Weekly Individual Productivity Pipeline 

## Overview  
This document outlines a 5-stage weekly individual productivity pipeline tailored for specific roles including Product Managers (PM), Developers (Dev), UX/UI Designers, Business Analysts (BA), and Quality Assurance (QA) teams. This pipeline is designed to streamline processes, classify efforts, and enhance overall productivity through the use of role-specific shortcuts and templates.

---

## 5-Stage Pipeline  
**Stage 1: Planning**  
- Define goals and objectives for the week.  
- Identify role-specific shortcuts.  
- Estimated time to setup: 1 hour.  

**Stage 2: Execution**  
- Implement tasks according to template.  
- Gather necessary inputs and resources.  

**Stage 3: Validation**  
- Assess production-validated evidence gate.  
- Check role-specific sourcing patterns.  
- Validation Requirements: Ensure all criteria are met before moving forward.

**Stage 4: Review**  
- Perform peer reviews and feedback sessions.  
- Assess efforts and time savings scoring.
  
**Stage 5: Reusability Classification**  
- Classify items as personal/team/cross-org.
- Document lessons learned and area improvements for future iterations.

2. Check for edge cases.  
3. Document test cases thoroughly.  
4. Ensure all required approvals are obtained.  
5. Full regression tests completed.  
6. Performance criteria met.  
7. Security practices followed.  
8. User journey tested.  
9. Errors documented and tracked.  
10. Cross-browser and device compatibility checked.  
11. Accessibility standards met.

15. Code review findings addressed.  
16. Backup plans in place.
